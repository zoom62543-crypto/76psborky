Accommodation

Sleeping Arrangements

Children are accommodated in traditional wooden tents with solid flooring. Each tent features 2 separate beds with foam mattresses and storage shelves. Tents are covered with waterproof tarps for complete weather protection.

Hygiene & Facilities

Sanitary Facilities

Our camp features multiple taps with running water for daily hygiene needs. Heated showers are available in a dedicated tent. Wooden latrines in a nearby grove serve as sanitary facilities.

Water Quality

Fresh water is delivered regularly from nearby Heraltice and meets all hygiene regulations. Our facilities undergo annual health inspections to ensure the highest standards of cleanliness.

Meals & Nutrition

Daily Meals

Our camp kitchen serves 5 nutritious meals daily, prepared by experienced cooks using fresh ingredients. The dining hall in our military hangar provides a comfortable space for meals.

Kitchen Duty

As part of the camp experience, children take turns with kitchen duties, learning valuable skills like teamwork and responsibility while helping with age-appropriate tasks.

Location & Environment

Natural Setting

Our camp is located in the beautiful Vysočina region, about 1 km past Hrutov village. Set on a meadow surrounded by forests, the location offers diverse outdoor activities. A nearby pond provides swimming opportunities, and the area is free from flood risk.
