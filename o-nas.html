Our Story

We are a dedicated team of enthusiasts who have been organizing children's summer camps near the village of Hrutov for over 20 years. Our passion for camps was awakened by our predecessors, who inspired us with their love of nature and adventure.

Today, we can't imagine summer without camp. Many of our current leaders started as campers themselves, creating a continuous tradition of quality and care that spans generations.

Certification & Standards

Pionýr Organization

We operate under the auspices of Pionýr, ensuring that all our staff are properly trained and certified. This means your children are in the hands of professionals who understand child development and safety.

Annual Inspections

Our facilities undergo regular health and safety inspections. We meet all hygiene guidelines and maintain the highest standards of cleanliness and care.
